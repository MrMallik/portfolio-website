### Plan

Components of the blog - 

- **landing page of the blog** where the blog entries are shown as thumbnails, clicking on each shall take to the individual blog post
- **Individual page of the blog post** with related post shown in and around the so the user can click and continue reading there
- A **admin management** page where new authors can be managed etc. This should serve as the central place of management
- A **blog compose** page to edit/add single blog post. It should be very minimal and should focus highly on great writing experience. The   writer should be top notch even allowing things like Quotes or references like Medium and Blogger. The editor should be rich.
